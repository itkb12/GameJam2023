[English description]

This font is based on the font True Crimes 
by the late Walter Velez (28 November 1939 - 24 August 2018).

Velez published it on his website in 2002 but he never really finished it
(his version included only basic letters and numbers).
Unfortunately the original site had disappeared and it seemed 
the font was never going to be finished so I decided to take
care of if and I revised the font.

I added the whole "Latin-1 Supplement" and "Latin Extended A" and I tweaked all margins and glyphs.
They are a bit more ballanced and smoother now (i.e. there are less points).

The upper case letters in the Unified version are redesigned to match the width of strokes of other characters.

If you think something in this font still doesn't feel right please contact me.
